# CycleBubble redesign notes

## Corrected direction

The product's highlight is the cycle bubble. The new design makes the bubble the main interface, not a decorative asset and not a generic health chart. The bubble should feel soft, slow, and alive.

## What the bubble means

- Liquid volume: how full the current state feels today.
- Liquid flow speed: how quickly the user's internal state is changing.
- Color: cycle phase and broad body rhythm, not a direct emotion label.
- Floating particles: body and life signals such as less sleep, many events, and a heavier body feeling.
- Nearby bubbles: anonymous resonance from similar feelings.

## De-labeling language

Avoid putting words like anxious, irritable, angry, depressed, or emotionally unstable on the home screen. These can intensify the user's state and make the product feel judgmental. Use softer observational language: "the bubble is fuller today", "the flow is slower", "some feelings may be closer to the surface", and "leave the day a little looser".

## Product boundary

CycleBubble should not compete with Apple Health or Meiyou as a chart/calendar product. Its difference is that it turns cycle and emotion into a single soft state object that users can read at a glance.

## Kept principles

- The first screen shows the user's own cycle bubble.
- The language stays non-diagnostic and non-judgmental.
- Emotion notes stay private.
- Anonymous resonance remains bounded: no follows, no private messages, no public comment feed.
